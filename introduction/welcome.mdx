Kleap is an AI website builder. Describe what you want in plain language and Kleap generates a fast, modern, SEO-optimized website — then publishes it live for you automatically.

## What is Kleap?

Kleap turns a conversation into a real, published website:

- **Build faster**: Go from idea to a live site in minutes
- **No coding required**: Describe your site in plain language
- **Auto-published**: Every generation goes live at `your-app.kleap.io` — no separate deploy step
- **Yours to grow**: Connect a custom domain, collect form submissions, and add a database when you need one

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Building">
    Describe your site in natural language. Kleap generates a fast, SEO-optimized site built on **Astro** and **Tailwind CSS**, using React islands for anything interactive.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Instant, Automatic Publishing">
    When a generation finishes, your site is published automatically to `your-app.kleap.io` on Cloudflare's global edge network — with free HTTPS and no build step to manage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Live Preview">
    See your site update as you build. Every change is reflected in a real, built preview.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom Domains">
    Connect your own domain with automatic, free SSL — or buy a new domain directly inside Kleap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Built-in Forms">
    Contact, lead, and newsletter forms work out of the box. Submissions land in your dashboard with email notifications — no setup required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Optional Database & Accounts">
    Need bookings, saved data, or user logins? Add **Kleap Database** — a built-in Postgres database with user accounts — when your app needs it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
