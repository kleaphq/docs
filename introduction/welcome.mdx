Kleap is an AI builder for websites **and web apps**. Describe what you want in plain language and Kleap generates a fast, modern, SEO-optimized site — or a full app with its own database and user login — then publishes it live for you automatically.

## What is Kleap?

Kleap turns a conversation into a real, published website — or a full web app:

- **Build faster**: Go from idea to a live site in minutes
- **No coding required**: Describe your site in plain language
- **Auto-published**: Every generation goes live at `your-app.kleap.io` — no separate deploy step
- **More than a site**: Connect a custom domain, collect form submissions, and add a built-in database with user logins for real app features

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Building">
    Describe your site in natural language. Kleap generates a fast, SEO-optimized site built on **Astro** and **Tailwind CSS**, using React islands for anything interactive.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Instant, Automatic Publishing">
    When a generation finishes, your site is published automatically to `your-app.kleap.io` on a global edge network — with free HTTPS and no build step to manage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Live Preview">
    See your site update as you build. Every change is reflected in a real, built preview.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Custom Domains">
    Connect your own domain with automatic, free SSL — or buy a new domain directly inside Kleap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Built-in Forms">
    Contact, lead, and newsletter forms work out of the box. Submissions land in your dashboard with email notifications — no setup required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Database, Accounts & Dashboards">
    Build real apps, not just sites. **Kleap Database** gives you a built-in database, user accounts and login, dashboards, and records — with per-user security — all generated by AI. Add it whenever your app needs to store, gate, or manage data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
