There are many AI website builders. Here's why entrepreneurs, designers, and developers choose Kleap.

## Key Differentiators

### Fast, SEO-Ready Output

Kleap builds real websites on a modern, performance-first stack:

- **Astro 5** — ships mostly static HTML, so pages load fast and rank well
- **Tailwind CSS v4** — clean, responsive design
- **React 19 islands** — interactivity only where you need it, without slowing the whole page
- **SEO & GEO optimized** — structured markup and metadata built in, so both Google and AI answer engines can read your site

### Real Publishing, Not Just Previews

Your site isn't trapped in a sandbox. With Kleap you get:

- **Automatic publishing** to `your-app.kleap.io` on a global edge network
- **Custom domains** with free, auto-renewing SSL
- **GitHub sync** to back up and own your code
- **Full access** to your source files and a built-in code editor

### Built-in Essentials

No extra services to wire up:

| Capability | Included |
|------------|----------|
| Contact & lead forms | Yes — submissions in your dashboard |
| Database & user accounts | Optional — add **Kleap Database** when needed |
| Analytics | Yes — visitors and pageviews |
| Images | Upload your own or generate with AI |
| Hosting & SSL | Yes — global edge, always on |

### Transparent Pricing

A simple credit-based system with no hidden fees:

- **Free tier** with daily credits to get started
- **Paid credits roll over** and never expire
- **No per-deployment charges** — publishing is always free
- See live plans at [kleap.co/pricing](https://kleap.co/pricing)
