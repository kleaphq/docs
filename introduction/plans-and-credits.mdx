Kleap uses a credit-based system. Credits are consumed when you use the AI to build and edit your sites. Publishing, hosting, and viewing your sites are always free.

<Info>
  Plans and prices below are current entry points. For the live, up-to-date pricing and credit sliders, visit [kleap.co/pricing](https://kleap.co/pricing).
</Info>

## Pricing Plans

| Plan | Price | Best for | Monthly AI credits |
|------|-------|----------|--------------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Trying Kleap, small sites | 5 free credits per day |
| **Launch** | $9/mo (or $99/yr) | Publishing with your own domain | Hosting only — keeps the 5 free daily credits |
| **Pro** | from $25/mo | Growing businesses | from 100 credits/mo (scalable) |
| **Business** | from $50/mo | Scaling teams | from 100 credits/mo (scalable) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large teams | Unlimited — [contact us](mailto:hello@kleap.co) |

### How Pro & Business scale

Pro and Business aren't fixed tiers — you pick how many monthly AI credits you need with a slider, and the price scales with it (from 100 credits up to 10,000+). **Business** includes everything in **Pro** plus team features: **SSO**, **design templates**, **private projects**, and the option to **opt out of data training**.

Paid plans bill **monthly or yearly** — yearly billing is discounted. See [kleap.co/pricing](https://kleap.co/pricing) for exact annual savings.

<Info>
  Every plan — including Free — includes **5 free AI credits per day**. **Launch** is a hosting plan: it unlocks a custom domain and always-on hosting, and keeps your daily free credits, but doesn't add a monthly credit pool.
</Info>

## How Credits Work

### Free daily credits

Every user can claim **5 free credits** once every 24 hours.

- **Claim frequency**: once per 24 hours
- **Credits per claim**: 5
- **Free-plan cap**: up to 30 claims per month

### Paid monthly credits

Paid plans add a monthly pool of AI credits on top of the daily free ones.

- **Paid credits roll over** — they never expire
- Free daily credits are used first, then your paid balance

### Credit consumption

Credits are **usage-based**, not a flat cost per message. A short tweak costs a fraction of a credit; a large, complex build costs more. As a rough guide:

| Action | Estimated Credits |
|--------|-------------------|
| Small text or style change | Under 1 credit |
| New section or component | 1–3 credits |
| Full page creation | 3–8 credits |
| Large, complex feature | 8+ credits |

<Info>
  **Image generation is free** — it doesn't consume credits. **Text editing** (clicking text in the preview and typing) is also free.
</Info>

### Credit top-ups

Need more credits without changing plans? You can buy one-time credit top-ups from your dashboard. First-time top-ups include a bonus.

### What happens when you run out?

- Your published sites keep working normally
- You can't make new AI edits until you get more credits
- Options: wait for your next daily claim, buy a top-up, upgrade your plan, or earn referral credits

## Referral Program

Earn bonus credits by inviting friends:

| Who | Credits Earned |
|-----|----------------|
| **You (referrer)** | +15 credits when your friend publishes their first site |
| **Your friend (referee)** | +10 credits on signup |

### How to Refer

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Copy your unique referral link
3. Share it with friends
4. They sign up and you both earn credits

## Free Actions

These never consume credits:

- Viewing and browsing your sites
- Publishing and hosting (always free)
- Connecting custom domains
- Editing text directly in the preview
- Generating images
- Viewing version history and the code editor
- Managing account and team settings
